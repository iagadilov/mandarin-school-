# Mandarin Stage 1 Acceptance Checklist

This checklist mirrors the agreed Stage 1 contract, WhatsApp brief, and methodist context.

## Included

- Mandarin branded online trainer.
- Main training flow shows numbers one by one.
- Full example is not visible before answer entry.
- Full example is revealed only after answer check.
- Student enters the final answer after the last number.
- The app checks the answer automatically.
- Result states: correct / incorrect.
- Incorrect result shows the correct answer.
- Final screen shows total examples, correct answers, errors, and percentage.
- Repeat and new training actions are available after the series.
- Rows setting: 2 to 10 numbers per example.
- Examples setting: 1 to 50 examples.
- Speed setting: 5.0 seconds to 0.1 seconds.
- Stage 1 blocks are present:
  - units without formulas, 1-9;
  - tens without formulas, 10-90;
  - identical double digits, 11-99;
  - three-digit numbers up to 999;
  - formulas on 5;
  - formulas on 10.
- Units without formulas follow the accepted direct-move rule:
  - `4 + 5 - 8 + 1` is valid;
  - `4 + 1`, `3 + 2`, `5 - 1`, `2 - 3` are invalid for this block.
- Tens, identical double digits, and three-digit blocks reuse the accepted direct-move logic by scaling.
- Design uses Mandarin red/orange/yellow as primary colors.
- Green is used only as a secondary success accent.
- Layout is responsive for phone, tablet, laptop, and wide desktop.

## Explicitly Outside Stage 1
